Redefining a Livestream E-commerce APP

EVALUATIVE RESEARCH | PRODUCT STRATEGY | MARKET RESEARCH

1.  THE CHALLENGE

One Piece is a livestream e-commerce app, focusing on the handicrafts, jewelry, jade, art collection and other non-standard commodities. Users of this product include both buyers and sellers. Despite the overall growth trend observed in the live streaming market, One Piece’s product team noticed that the APP’s user acquisition and retention rates were not growing. Therefore, our team was invited to reassess the product strategy.

My Role

Product Strategy Intern

Objective

Build stickiness to retain users and improve brand loyalty by enhancing user experience.

Teams

Strategy team(our team)

One Piece Product Team

Data Team 

2.  RESEARCH PROCESS

Phase 1 User Research

Phone Interview On Lost Users

To find out why our users were leaving us, we conducted semi-structured interviews on lost users to understand their attitudes, needs and objectives. Questions from the interview guide include: Why did you download/uninstall our app? Have you bought anything before, why or why not? Aside from One Piece, are there any other purchase channels (competitors’ products) that you have experience with? … I coded interview responses and performed descriptive analysis.

From the analysis of interview data, we generated insights on user behaviors and pain points from both buyers and sellers’ ends.

 Insights from Buyers

  • Buyers of non-standard products prefer to buy offline, and more than half of them also use mobile Taobao.
  • Positive feedback is mainly on the product function, half of the negative feedback is on quality control.
  • The accuracy of advertising is low, with target users accounting for only 30%.

 Insights from Sellers

  • Most merchants of our platform are also on other e-commerce platforms, among which Taobao accounts for the highest proportion..
  • Positive reviews are mainly on the live broadcast function, negative reviews focus on low traffic or lack of new customers.

Buyer Personas

We noticed that buyers were critical to One Piece and created personas based on user data and interviews to build empathy and gain perspectives.

Phase 2 Strategy Development 

In-depth Interviews/Talks with Experts 

In light of the findings in the user research phase, we consulted several successful merchants and experts in the non-standard products e-commerce field, aiming at developing effective operation/marketing strategies, and a user-centric business mode.

Experts believe in ...

Customer Relationship: Relying on word-of-mouth to fission customers; Use live streaming to build trust and emotional bonds.

Self-generated Content: Educate and communicate knowledge of products with the buyers during the live streaming.

Other Suggestions: Provide training on live streaming skills and instant message function to enable communication.

Competitive Analysis

In the meanwhile, we conducted competitive analysis to gain a broader understanding of the market landscape.

3.  INSIGHTS AND BUSINESS OUTCOMES

Through a series of primary and secondary research, we learned that the nature of non-standard products determined the central role of customer trust in products, merchants and the platform. Trust is the key to conversion rate, and responsibility is the key to repurchase rate. Ultimately, long-term sustainable development gains are achieved through trust and responsibility.

On this basis, we believe that the use of social relationship chain to attract, maintain and manage users could positively influence the conversion rate.
