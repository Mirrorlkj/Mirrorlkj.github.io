Hi, I’m Mirror!

A forever curious social observer with 3 years of academic and industry experience researching human. I empathize with others by uncovering motives, emotions, perceptions and other cognitive processes happening behind behaviors and attitudes.

I recently graduated from MA in Social Sciences at the University of Chicago with a concentration on Psychology and Quantitative Methods (STEM) and aspire to start my career as a UX Researcher!

Previously, I conducted independent survey-based research at Attention, Perception and Experience(APEX) Lab, investigated consumer insights for digital marketing at L’Oréal, developed strategies and experience for a variety of mobile products at JOYY (a global video-based social media platform listed on the NASDAQ).

 My background in Business Administration allows me to think strategically, translate business requirements into researchable UX questions and then turn the findings into impactful business decisions.
