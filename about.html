Hi, I’m Mirror

A forever curious social observer with 3 years of academic and industry experience studying humans. I empathize with others by uncovering motives, emotions, perceptions and other cognitive processes behind behaviors and attitudes. 

I recently graduated from MA in Social Sciences (MAPSS) at the UChicago with concentrations on Cog Psych and Quant Methods and aspire to start my career as a UX Researcher!

Over the past months, I’ve been working on 3 UX projects with XFT, including 1 with a startup, 1 with an NGO, and the other with an HCI Lab. I’m unstoppable in learning, developing and implementing mixed methods to humanize and democratize tech.

In addition, I’ve cultivated strategic thinking from being a business student for 4 years, a product strategy analyst and a consumer insights researcher. I’ve also gained technical and statistical skills from analyzing different data formats using SQL, excel, R and SPSS.
